Biomarker

Coronary artery calcification and cardiovascular outcomes — machine learning

  • Build prediction models on CAC density and volume using elastic net and random forest.
  • Quantify added value over clinical baselines with discrimination and reclassification metrics; account for competing risk with Fine–Gray.
  • Deliverables include feature sets, validated models, and clear figures for interpretation.

Proteomics and valvular calcification — ARIC and MESA

  • Use large-scale proteomics from ARIC for discovery and MESA for replication to identify proteins linked to aortic or mitral valve calcification.
  • Adjust for covariates and control multiple testing; assemble candidate protein panels and summarize biological pathways.
  • Evaluate incremental prediction beyond clinical variables and imaging markers.

Valvular calcification and valvular dysfunction

  • Relate CT-derived valvular calcification to echocardiographic function such as aortic stenosis.
  • Fit spline-based linear and logistic models; report discrimination with AUC and document calibration and sensitivity checks.
  • Produce subgroup analyses and ready-to-use graphics for clinicians.

Nutrition

Healthy food procurement — Nepal school survey

  • Evaluate pre- to post-policy changes in school food environments and trans-fat availability.
  • Apply linear mixed models, mixed-effects logistic regression, and cumulative logistic mixed-effects models.
  • Translate results into policy-ready summaries with clear visualizations.

Low-sodium salt substitute — Bangladesh trial (participants on antihypertensive therapy)

  • Design questionnaires and build the REDCap data system with full documentation.
  • Monitor feasibility and safety signals to support implementation planning.
  • Streamline data flow and quality control for multi-site coordination.

Low-sodium salt substitute — research prioritization

  • Develop and field a priority-setting survey across stakeholders.
  • Curate responses, visualize findings, and help write the research roadmap.
  • Identify short- and mid-term trials most likely to impact population health.

Hypertension

Community-based HEARTS pilot — Bangladesh

  • Monitor longitudinal follow-up data and lead analyses of medication changes, retention, blood pressure control, and trajectories.
  • Create concise dashboards and briefs to support program decisions and scale-up.
  • Standardize reporting templates for implementers and partners.

Peripheral Artery Disease

Prevalence and risk factors of PAD in Hispanics

  • Combine multimodality measures to estimate PAD prevalence and key risk factors.
  • Run stratified and interaction analyses to keep outputs clinically interpretable.
  • Deliver subgroup summaries and figures that highlight actionable disparities.

New diagnostic criteria for PAD — validity

  • Compare sensitivity, specificity, and agreement of proposed versus existing thresholds across risk strata.
  • Quantify diagnostic performance with ROC-based metrics and agreement statistics.
  • Provide clear guidance on cut-points suited to different clinical settings.

Kidney measures and PAD outcomes

  • Use KDIGO categories to study independent and interaction effects of kidney measures on PAD outcomes.
  • Apply stratified and interaction models with transparent effect displays.
  • Summarize implications for screening and risk communication.
